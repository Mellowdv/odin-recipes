Chicken Teriyaki

Description

Sweet, salty, slightly spicy and wonderfully full - chicken teriyaki for two.

Ingredients

  • 0.5kg chicken breast fillet
  • Rice, as much as you want
  • Two teaspoons of rice vinegar or lime juice
  • Four tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Two tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1-2 tablespoons of honey
  • Teaspoon of shredded ginger
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic
  • A medium sized onion

Preparation

  1. Slice the chicken into thin strips
  2. Peel and shred ginger
  3. Grind the garlic
  4. Slice the onion
  5. Mix the oil, vinegar, soy sauce and honey on the pan
  6. Heat the mixture up and add ginger and onion
  7. After 3-5 minutes add garlic and start preparing rice
  8. Bring the sauce to a boil and add the meat
  9. Stir it regularly, switch sides every 5 minutes for a total of 15 minutes
  10. Remember to use copious amounts of sauce and large helpings of rice
  11. Enjoy!
