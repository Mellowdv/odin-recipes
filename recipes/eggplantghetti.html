Eggplantghetti

Description

A simple dish made out of relatively few ingredients invented by my girlfriend, no one can convince me otherwise.

Perfect for busy people and students. Mix of slightly spicy and sweet thanks to the combination of honey and garlic.

Ingredients (for two people)

  • A small eggplant or half of a bigger one (duh)
  • Pasta of your liking (around 2 cups)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Honey (2 tablespoons>
  • Salt (3-4 pinches)
  • Ground black pepper (1-2 pinches)
  • Powdered red bell pepper (1 teaspoon)
  • Powdered smoked red bell pepper (1/2 a teaspoon)
  • Turmeric (1 pinch)
  • Ginger (about 1 teaspoon)
  • Tomato paste
  • Around 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small onion

Preparation

  1. Cut up the eggplant the way you like it
  2. Put the eggplant in a bowl and add plenty of salt
  3. Leave the eggplant for 15 - 20 minutes, the salt will make it less bitter
  4. Cut up the onion, garlic and ginger
  5. Mix the olive oil with the spices (powdered red bell peppers, ground black pepper, tumeric)
  6. Once the 15 - 20 minutes are up, rinse the eggplant with water and dry it
  7. Add the olive with spices and honey to the eggplant
  8. Start frying the eggplant
  9. After 3-5 minutes add onion
  10. After another 3~ minutes add garlic and ginger
  11. While frying the eggplant, boil the water for the pasta
  12. When the eggplant looks a bit brown, add tomato paste
  13. Boil until the paste becomes thick enough for your liking
  14. Once the pasta and the sauce with eggplant are ready mix them together
  15. Enjoy!
