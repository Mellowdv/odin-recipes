Wonder Pasta

Description

Simple recipe for busy or lazy days

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Basil or tomato pesto
  • Black olives
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Prosciutto ham
  • Olive oil (the fresher, the better)

Preparation

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Slice the onion and garlic
  3. Fry the onion and garlic on a pan
  4. Add sliced ham, let it fry for a bit
  5. Add large amounts of pesto
  6. Let it come to a boil
  7. Remember to take care of the pasta!
  8. Mix the freshly boiled pasta with the sauce
  9. Add olives
  10. Add olive oil to taste
  11. Enjoy!
